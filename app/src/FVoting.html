How to use the DApp:

Step 1: Install the metamask plugin and create an account on it and load some Ether.

Step 2: Purchase tokens below by entering the total number of tokens you like to buy.

Step 3: Vote for candidates by entering their name of weight to vote with.

Step 4: Enter your account address to look up your voting activity.
